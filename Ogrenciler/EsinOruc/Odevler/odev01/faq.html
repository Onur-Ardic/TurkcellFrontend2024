Frequently Asked Questions

Do stones have memories?

In a geological sense, stones can be said to "record" the Earth's history. They can hold information about the environment in which they were formed, such as the temperature, pressure, and chemical conditions that existed at the time. This "record" can be read by geologists through methods such as radiometric dating, analysis of mineral composition, and studying the layers of sedimentary rocks. So in this way, stones can provide a kind of "memory" of the Earth's past.

Do natural stones have energy?

The energy of natural stones is often a common concept in metaphysical and spiritual belief systems. In crystal healing, energy work and some spiritual practices, it is thought that stones and minerals have certain vibrations and that these vibrations can positively affect people's energy fields. It is believed that each of the stones has different properties and provides various spiritual, mental or physical benefits.

Which natural stone should I buy?

The natural stone you are about to choose may vary depending on your wishes and the meaning and benefits the stone can give you. If you are looking for peace and tranquility, amethyst, known for its purple tones and believed to give a feeling of serenity, may be an ideal choice. Known as the stone of love and compassion and attracting attention with its gentle pink tone, rose quartz will be a great choice if you want to strengthen your relationships. For your business and career goals, yellow citrine, which is associated with increasing motivation and creativity, may be suitable for you.

Is there a money stone?

While these stones are associated with wealth and prosperity in various metaphysical belief systems, there is no scientific evidence to support the claim that any stone can directly influence financial fortune. However, many people enjoy these stones for their beauty and the positive psychological effects they can bring, such as a reminder to maintain a mindset of abundance and prosperity.
